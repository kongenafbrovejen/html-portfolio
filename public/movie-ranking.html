The Rock

Starring: Sean Connery, Ed Harris, Nicholas Cage
Hummel (Harris), takes prisoners at Alcatraz - holds the city of Los Angeles at ransom threatning to use rockets loaded with VX poisen gas. Stanley Goodspeed (Cage) is an biotechnician at FBI, gets the call to stop Hummel, being a "office" biotech. he is not equiped to handle this situation on his own, he is getting help from a convict called Mason (Connery), who is still locked up in prison. Mason has once been successfull of escaping Alcatraz therefor the FBI needs him to help them access the island and stop Hummel.

The Usual Suspects
